Face Detection App

Welcome to the Face Detection App!

This application allows you to detect faces in images.

For more reference on the methods used, please refer to my research paper on Link To Paper

Instructions:

  • Choose an image:
    • You can either upload your own image using the "Upload Image" button.
    • Or, you can click on the "Sample Images" button to choose from a set of preloaded sample images.
  • Click on the "Detect Faces" button to perform face detection on the selected image.
  • The detected faces will be highlighted with bounding boxes.
